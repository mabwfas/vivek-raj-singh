NidhiPay
AI-Powered UPI & Banking Super-App
628K Downloads
Client: KisanCredit (YC W23)
Role: Sole Developer & Architect
Timeline: 4.5 months
Platforms: iOS & Android

⚠ The Problem

KisanCredit, a YC-backed agritech startup, needed a UPI payment app for rural Indian farmers. Their users had low-end devices with 1GB RAM, unreliable 2G/3G connectivity, and zero trust in digital payments after failed transactions on competitor apps. Existing fintech solutions crashed on budget devices and had no offline capability — causing 40%+ transaction failure rates.

✅ My Solution

I designed and built an offline-first Flutter fintech super-app from scratch. Created a custom payment orchestration layer with 3-gateway failover (Razorpay, PhonePe, Paytm). Added AI expense categorization and biometric security — all optimized for Android 5.0+ devices with 1GB RAM.

Complete Flutter app (iOS & Android) with 22 screens

Case Study 05
DabbaRun
Hyperlocal Food Delivery Platform
89K Monthly Orders
Client: Personal Project (Startup)
Role: Solo Developer & Architect
Timeline: 3 months
Platforms: iOS & Android (3 apps)

⚠ The Problem

Home-cooked food delivery in tier-2/tier-3 Indian cities had no reliable digital platform. Swiggy and Zomato didn't serve smaller cities, and home chefs had no way to reach customers. The platform needed to handle complex irregular delivery zones, support 8 Indian languages, and work reliably on low-bandwidth networks — all with three separate user-facing apps (customer, chef, delivery partner).

✅ My Solution

Built a full-stack hyperlocal delivery platform with a Flutter monorepo powering 3 separate apps (customer, chef, delivery) from shared packages. Engineered a custom delivery routing algorithm that's 22% faster than Google Maps' default routing for short hyperlocal distances. Created a geofencing engine for irregularly shaped delivery zones and added 8-language i18n support.

📦 What I Delivered

3 Flutter apps (customer, chef, delivery) from shared monorepo
Real-time order tracking with live map and dynamic ETA
Custom routing algorithm (22% faster than Google Maps default)
Subscription meal plans with weekly menu customization
Multi-language support in 8 Indian languages
Custom geofencing for irregularly shaped delivery zones
Chef dashboard with menu management, earnings & analytics
